Find your NHS number

Use this form to get your NHS number.

Your NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 999 123 4567.

Who can use this form

You need to be registered with a GP in England to use this form, as we match your details with your GP record.

You can use this form for yourself or for someone else.

How you will get your NHS number

You can choose how you receive your NHS number

Depending on the details your GP has, you can choose from:

  • email
  • text message
  • letter

Before you start

You will need your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • postcode of the address you used to register with your GP surgery.
