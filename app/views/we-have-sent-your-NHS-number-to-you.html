When you will get your NHS number

Text message or email

It should arrive within 1 hour.

Letter

It should arrive within 5 days.

If it does not arrive

If you do not receive your NHS number after 5 days, contact your GP surgery.

If you do not know which GP surgery you are registered with, you can find a GP surgery here.

Other ways to get your NHS number

You can also find your NHS number by logging into the NHS website or NHS app.

Or you can create an account if you do not have one.
